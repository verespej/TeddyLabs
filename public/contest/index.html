Teddy Labs is having a contest!

Win a $50 Amazon gift card and a toy custom-made for your child!

We want to stimulate your child's creativity while making playtime more fun. To do so, we're giving away a great prize package to each of 2 winners. Entering is easy. All you have to do is:
  1. Have your child think of his or her favorite characters from a cartoon, video game, mobile phone game, or book. Choose a character that you haven't seen in toy stores.
  2. Have your child draw the character on paper. Feel free to include accessories and anything else your child dreams up. Color is not required, but is preferred.
  3. Take a photo of the drawing and post it to the Teddy Labs Facebook page or Tweet it to "@TeddyLabs #MakeMyToy". If you don't have a Facebook or Twitter account, email the photo to hakon@teddylabs.com.
  4. That's it!
Submissions must be in by 11:59pm PDT on Monday, September 30, 2013. We'll review the submissions and announce the 2 winners at 12pm on Friday, October 4, 2013.

Each winner will receive a $50 Amazon gift card and we will create a custom toy for your child based on the drawing he or she made.

We can't wait to see what you and your child create!
